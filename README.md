## About This Portfolio

This repository showcases analytical work developed through **Pharmetric LLC**, an independent BI and analytics consulting practice. The projects here apply statistical modeling, simulation, and measurement frameworks to pharmaceutical pricing — demonstrating capabilities in financial modeling, risk quantification, and data-driven pricing strategy.

These projects are directly relevant to Strategic Accounts pricing work in pharmaceutical distribution — including forward-looking margin modeling, pricing risk assessment, and measuring the impact of pricing decisions with statistical rigor.
